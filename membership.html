Membership Class

Take the next step in joining our church family

Become a Member

Membership at St. Simon Baptist Church represents a commitment to grow together as disciples of Jesus Christ. Our membership class will help you understand our church's history, beliefs, and mission, and how you can be part of what God is doing here.

1

Attend Class

Complete our 2-session membership class offered quarterly on Saturday mornings.

2

Share Your Story

Meet with a pastor or leader to share your testimony of faith in Christ.

3

Be Baptized

If you haven't been baptized by immersion since coming to faith, we'll schedule your baptism.

4

Be Welcomed

New members are introduced to the congregation during a Sunday service.
