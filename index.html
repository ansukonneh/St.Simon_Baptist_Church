Our Church Family

At St. Simon Baptist Church, we are a family of believers committed to serving God and our community. Founded in faith and grounded in the Word, we strive to be a light in our neighborhood and beyond. Whether you're seeking spiritual growth, meaningful fellowship, or a place to serve, you'll find a warm welcome here.

Our doors are open to everyone, and we believe that through worship, prayer, and service, we can transform lives and communities for Christ.
