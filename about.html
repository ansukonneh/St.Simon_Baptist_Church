Our Story & Beliefs

At Saint Simon we are a Family of Faith... Bound together in Love and strenghtened through Fellowship.

Our History

For over 100 years, St. Simon Baptist Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant congregation that continues to impact lives through the power of the Gospel.

1923

Church Founding

St. Simon Baptist Church was established by Rev. Dr. Daniel R. Horton and 12 founding members in a small storefront on Horton Avenue.

1965

First Building

The congregation moved into its first dedicated church building, which seated 150 worshippers.

1988

Expansion

Under the leadership of Pastor S. Rufus W. Reeves, the church expanded with new educational wings and a larger sanctuary.

2005

Community Center

The church opened its community outreach center, offering food programs, job training, and youth activities.

Present

Continuing Legacy

Today, under the leadership of Rev. Rufus Reeves, we continue to serve our community while staying true to our biblical foundations.

Our Beliefs

We hold to the historic Christian faith as revealed in the Bible. These core beliefs guide everything we do as a church family.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and imminent return.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone, not by works.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and make disciples.

Baptism

We believe in believer's baptism by immersion as an act of obedience and public testimony.

Church Membership

At St. Simon Baptist Church, we believe membership is more than just a name on a list - it's a commitment to grow together as the body of Christ. We invite you to join our church family as we pursue God's purpose together.

Becoming a Member

We offer quarterly membership classes that cover our church history, beliefs, and expectations. These classes are required for all prospective members.

  • Attend a membership class
  • Share your testimony of faith
  • Be baptized by immersion (if not previously baptized)
  • Complete membership application

Member Responsibilities

As members of St. Simon Baptist Church, we commit to:

  • Faithfully attend worship services
  • Grow spiritually through Bible study and prayer
  • Serve in a ministry according to your gifts
  • Give regularly and cheerfully
  • Live according to biblical principles
  • Support the church's mission and leadership

Member Benefits

As part of our church family, you'll receive:

  • Pastoral care and spiritual guidance
  • Opportunities to serve and use your gifts
  • Voting privileges in church matters
  • Access to member-only events and resources
  • The support of a spiritual family
