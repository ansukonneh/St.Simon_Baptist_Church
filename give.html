Giving FAQs

Is online giving secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption technology to protect your information. Your financial data is never stored on our servers.

Will I receive a giving statement?

Yes, we provide annual giving statements for tax purposes. You can also log in to your account at any time to view your giving history.

Can I set up recurring giving?

Absolutely. During the giving process, you'll have the option to make your gift recurring (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).

How much should I give?

The Bible encourages us to give cheerfully and sacrificially. Many members follow the biblical principle of tithing (giving 10%), but the amount is between you and God.
