Special Services

Communion Service

First Sunday of each month

We observe the Lord's Supper together as a church family during our morning service.

Quarterly

Celebration of new believers following Christ in believer's baptism by immersion.

Encounter Service

Week of prayer and fasting to seek God's direction for the new month.

What to Expect

A Warm Welcome

From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted with smiles and hospitality. We have friendly greeters ready to help you find your way.

Spirit-Filled Worship

Our services feature vibrant worship blending traditional hymns and contemporary praise songs led by our choir and worship team.

Biblical Preaching

Each service includes practical, Bible-based teaching that applies God's Word to everyday life.
